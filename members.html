For Prospective Ph.D. Students

I am looking for self-motivated Ph.D. students starting Spring/Fall-2023 to work on security and privacy topics in blockchain, distributed systems, next-G mobile networks, and computer/communication networks in general. If you are interested in working me, feel free to send an email to xiaoy[AT]uky[DOT]edu with your CV, transcripts, and a few sentences describing your research interests and expectation. There are two degree program choices: Ph.D. in Computer Science and Ph.D. in Computer Engineering, both within the CS Department while nuanced in curriculum. Please follow the respective application procedure/deadline and mention Dr. Yang Xiao in your statement.
