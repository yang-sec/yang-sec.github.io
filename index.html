About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Kentucky. My research area is Network and Information Security. I am particularly interested in security and privacy issues in computer/communication networks, distributed systems, blockchain and decentralized systems, and more recently next-G mobile networks. I enjoy analyzing the fundamental security and privacy challenges in these systems and developing novel solutions under the assumption of zero or limited trust. I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech, supervised by Dr. Wenjing Lou. I received my master's degree from University of Michigan in 2017 and bachelor's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2014.
